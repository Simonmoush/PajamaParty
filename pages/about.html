About

Welcome to the coziest zine on the internet. Pajama Party is a place for drawings, photos, videos, poetry, prose, music, and anything that can be shared through the web. Pajama Party is a place to tell jokes and secrets and hopes. Pajama Party is a place for friends. Please take your time and enjoy our first issue.
